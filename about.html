    Rachel can be found observing new blooms, drawing things around her, drinking tea or espresso while design thinking, photographing in 35mm, and writing the world's movements.

    Her curiousity and passion for culture and innovation continuously motivates her to travel and seek novel connections and knowledge. It's why she likes to ask memorable questions, make her own jewelry, learn new languages, and travel 5200m above sea level (a.k.a. Mt. Everest base camp level).

    She is a native of Hong Kong and Chicago (and partially Toronto), and studied at the University of British Columbia for a Bachelor of Commerce in Marketing and Sustainability.

    The little projects showcased are how she uses creativity and curiousity to connect with her environment through different perspectives and medium.
